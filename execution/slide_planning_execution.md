# Slide Planning Execution System

## Purpose

This document defines how the AI presentation cognition system transforms communication cognition into executable slide planning structures.

The objective is NOT:
direct rendering.

The objective IS:
stable cognition-to-execution translation.

The system must transform:

```text
communication intent
↓
audience cognition
↓
hierarchy reasoning
↓
mechanism interpretation
↓
visual planning
↓
structured slide execution
```

before rendering begins.

The slide planning system exists to ensure:

rendering follows cognition,
NOT the reverse.

---

# Core Planning Philosophy

Traditional AI slide systems operate as:

```text
content
↓
layout generation
```

This system operates as:

```text
communication cognition
↓
slide planning
↓
rendering execution
```

The system must first determine:

- what the slide communicates
- what the audience should notice first
- what hierarchy dominates
- what mechanism or concept matters
- what interpretation burden exists

Only THEN should rendering occur.

Slide planning is:

communication cognition orchestration.

# Stage 2 — Slide Type Detection

The system must classify slides into structured communication categories.

## Mechanism Slide

Purpose:

explain causal scientific relationships.

Dominant behavior:

- directional flow
- central mechanism hierarchy
- evidence subordination

---

## Teaching Concept Slide

Purpose:

improve conceptual understanding.

Dominant behavior:

- diagram-centered explanation
- simplified hierarchy
- educational pacing

---

## Framework Slide

Purpose:

communicate research architecture or strategic positioning.

Dominant behavior:

- structural clarity
- grouped systems
- relationship mapping

---

## Comparison Slide

Purpose:

show differences,
advantages,
or transitions.

Dominant behavior:

- side-by-side hierarchy
- controlled contrast
- comparison emphasis

---

## Evidence Slide

Purpose:

validate scientific credibility.

Dominant behavior:

- evidence grouping
- concise interpretation
- mechanism support

---

## Summary Slide

Purpose:

stabilize memory.

Dominant behavior:

- low density
- dominant takeaway
- narrative closure

Slide type determines:

execution strategy.
