## Scenario

Scientific conference presentation.

Topic:

Membrane-controlled irrigation
regulates rhizosphere nitrogen cycling.

Audience:

- researchers
- graduate students
- interdisciplinary agricultural scientists

Presentation Goal:

Explain how membrane-controlled irrigation
improves nitrogen utilization efficiency
through rhizosphere microenvironment regulation.

- what mechanism changes
- why nitrogen efficiency improves
- what evidence supports the mechanism
- why the research matters scientifically

Before finalizing:

Check:

- Is the dominant mechanism immediately obvious?
- Is causal direction visually clear?
- Is evidence hierarchically subordinate?
- Is whitespace sufficient?
- Is cognitive density controlled?
- Does the slide feel editorially calm?
