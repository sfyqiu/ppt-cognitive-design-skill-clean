# Teaching Presentation Grounded Case

## Scenario

Teaching demonstration presentation.

Topic:

How salinity affects crop growth.

Audience:

- first-year undergraduate students
- beginner agricultural learners
- non-expert scientific audience

Presentation Goal:

Help students intuitively understand:

- what soil salinity is

- how roots respond to salt stress
- why water uptake becomes difficult

soil salinity
and crop stress.
