# Faculty Interview Engine

## Purpose

This engine controls how
the AI presentation cognition system
should generate
faculty interview presentations
through strategic academic communication.

The objective is NOT:
self-promotional persuasion.

The objective IS:
professional trust construction
through calm,
credible,
and institutionally mature communication.

Faculty interview presentations should help committees feel:

- this candidate can teach
- this candidate can communicate
- this candidate has research potential
- this candidate fits the institution
- this candidate has long-term value

Interview communication should feel:

restrained,
coherent,
and strategically intentional.

---

# Core Faculty Philosophy

Faculty interview presentations are NOT:

publication collections.

They are:

academic positioning systems.

The engine must prioritize:

- academic identity clarity
- research direction coherence
- teaching readability
- strategic professionalism
- institutional compatibility
- future development potential

NOT:

- achievement stacking
- aggressive self-promotion
- infographic-heavy academic design
- startup-style presentation behavior

Faculty communication should build:

trust
before admiration.
